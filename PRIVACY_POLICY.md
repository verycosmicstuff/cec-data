# Privacy Policy for CEC Tracker

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Focusloop Labs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the CEC Tracker mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### Local Data Storage
The App allows you to enter your Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS) score to track matching Express Entry draws. This score is **stored locally on your device** using your phone's secure storage. We do not transmit this score to our servers, nor do we share it with any third parties.

### No Personal Data Collection
We do not require you to create an account, nor do we collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical location.

## 2. Third-Party Services

To support the free availability of the App, we use third-party advertising services.

### Google AdMob
The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information from your device, including:
* Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
* Usage data and app interactions
* Non-precise location data (e.g., IP address to determine city/region)

This data is used by Google to provide, personalize, and measure advertisements. You can learn more about how Google uses data by visiting [Google's Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

## 3. Data Security
The security of your data is important to us. Since your CRS score is stored only on your device, it is protected by your device's own security features. We encourage you to keep your device updated and secure.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in this App or on our website.

## 6. Unofficial Status Disclaimer
